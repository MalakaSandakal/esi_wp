Who We Are

ESI designs, engineers and manufactures complete, resilient energy solutions, providing you with the ability to create, store and manage your energy. Our Interactive Energy Grid (IEG) is the first and only fully integrated residential energy solution that keeps your power and heat on, no matter what. Our intelligent solution is built with everything needed to seamlessly capture, create, store, manage and distribute your electric and thermal energy. -No matter what the energy source—solar, wind, fuel-cell, co-generation, back-up power generation or public grid - our IEG unites them all.

As a member of your design team, we work with your Architect, Builder and other stakeholders to define your project's specific requirements.

These solutions may include Micro-Grids, Thermal-Grids, Energy Sources and Energy Storage Systems that are designed and engineered by ESI. We offer Project Coordination Services as needed for the project.

What We Do differently

Problem: Ignoring thermal energy needs ​

Typical Solar + Battery solutions fail to address the thermal energy demands of a typical residence. The thermal component (DHW, HVAC, pool/spa) is approximately 60% of residential energy use. While those others may keep your lights on they won’t keep you comfortable.

ESI’s solution: ​

Our Interactive Energy Grid™ systems are a complete energy system delivering both the electrical and thermal energy necessary to keep your home secure and comfortable.
